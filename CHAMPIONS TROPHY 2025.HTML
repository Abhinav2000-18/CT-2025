Match 1: India vs Bangladesh

Toss: Bangladesh won the toss and elected to bat first.

First Innings: Bangladesh posted 228 runs with Towhid Hridoy scoring a century (100). Mohammed Shami's five-wicket haul restricted Bangladesh.

Second Innings: India chased the target in 46.3 overs, thanks to Shubman Gill's unbeaten 101.

Match 2: India vs Pakistan

Toss: Pakistan won the toss and chose to bat.

First Innings: Pakistan struggled and was bowled out for 241 runs.

Second Innings: Virat Kohli led India's chase with an unbeaten 100, securing a six-wicket win.

Match 3: India vs New Zealand

Toss: India won the toss and opted to bat.

First Innings: India managed 249/9, with New Zealand’s Matt Henry taking 5 wickets.

Second Innings: Varun Chakravarthy’s five-wicket haul bowled out New Zealand for 204, leading to a 44-run victory for India.

Semi-Final: India vs Australia

Toss: Australia won the toss and elected to bat.

First Innings: Australia posted 264 runs, with Mohammed Shami taking crucial wickets.

Second Innings: Virat Kohli’s 84 guided India to victory in 48.1 overs.

Final: India vs New Zealand

Toss: New Zealand won the toss and chose to bat.

First Innings: New Zealand scored 251/7, with Daryl Mitchell (63) and Michael Bracewell (53*) contributing.

Second Innings: Rohit Sharma (76) led the chase, with key contributions from Shreyas Iyer and KL Rahul. India won by four wickets to lift the trophy.

Celebration: The Indian team erupted in joy, lifting the trophy amid fireworks and chants from the fans.
